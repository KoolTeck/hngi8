SKILLS SET

These are the qualities you should posses to get through this internship successfully.

Communication: Your ability to write and speak professionally. To convey ideas and information effectively.


Interpersonal: This is often refered to as “people skills”. It is your ability to interact with others, to be able build and maintain relationships, to be the kind of person people would like to work with.


Collaboration: As an intern, it is very likely that you will be asked to work with other interns. You should have the ability to be a team player and possess the capacity to work with others to achieve a common goal.


Critical Thinking: This refers to your ability to evaluate a system or an issue and come up with a solution. HNG interns must be critical thinkers


Adaptability: As a HNG intern, you should be able to adapt to different kinds of situations i.e you should be ready to learn from other areas of practice as well.
